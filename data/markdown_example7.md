# CERN's Collaborative Model

CERN operates as an international laboratory for particle physics research. Scientists from multiple countries contribute to experiments requiring enormous computational analysis.

The collaborative culture at CERN created both technical and organizational innovations. The World Wide Web emerged directly from the need to share research efficiently across institutional boundaries.

**Structured Overview**
- Type: International research organization
- Focus: Particle physics
- Founded: 1954
- Location: Geneva, Switzerland
