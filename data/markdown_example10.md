# Interdependence of Space and Networks

Modern space missions depend on global communication networks to transmit telemetry and scientific data. High-speed Internet infrastructure allows researchers to analyze results almost instantly.

Without early computing theory, ARPANET’s networking principles, or NASA’s embedded systems development during Apollo, this level of integration would not exist.
